Family Symbol Intention

Our family symbol came from a letter Tim Ryan wrote to the family during a difficult time. In this letter, later named the family dedication, he charged the older generation with being guardians over the rest of the family, each other and especially the younger generations. He charged the younger generation also as guardians of each other. You can find this letter here.

The Process for getting the Family Symbol.

If you're blood its as simple as requesting access to the secure drive folder with the artwork. Then following the Intention of the symbol. We've designed this symbol not only with the existing members of our family in mind but all of our family whom have passed and all of our future family yet to come.

If you have someone who is not blood, keep the following in mind. This is for people who are family in our hearts and mind and if someone were to mention this particular person to someone else it would be obvious to us all that they are indeed family. If you have further questions about this please contact Bill Martin or Tim Ryan.

The Meaning and Rules of the Family Symbol Tattoo.

The leaf is to symbolize each of us individually on the family tree, as we each make up a leaf on the family tree. The tree in itself is what we consider to be our family, whether distant or immediate, blood or like blood we are all as one. The double infinity symbol is infinity times infinity, there is nothing greater than that just like there is nothing greater than the love we have for one another. It is an infinite amount of love and responsibility and honor we hold for one another and nothing can compare to our family. Nothing. It's a symbol that ties us all together no matter the amount of miles that keeps us apart.

Keeping the integrity of this symbol is critical. If we don't hold true to the intention of the symbol its meaning will lessen for all of us. With this in mind we've established the following rules for the symbol.

You may put the symbol anywhere you would like, in any size you would like; However, we recommend that you place it somewhere you will see it every day and be reminded that you're part of a family that loves you and cares about you and like the tattoo is permanent in your life and will always be there for you.

You may only get the symbol using the stencils we provide to you. Also, The tattoo may only be in black. You may use any tattoo artist you wish, and should use the pictures posted as the tattoo artists' guide for proper shading. The intention is that when together we all have the same "brand" of our family. When others see this symbol, We want them to know that this is something special not just a cool tattoo or idea someone had one day. We wouldn't change the colors of the flag, nor will we change the design or color of our family symbol.
